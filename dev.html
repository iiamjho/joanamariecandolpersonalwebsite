      CHILDHOOD

         PUBERTY
I consider puberty as the thrilliest stage of mine.
Why? Because in this stage I've learned how to make
lies to my parents just for me to be able to go to the
mall with my friends. In this stage, I am starting to
become aware of what is happening within myself and
around me.











      ADOLESCENCE
Currently, I am on adolescence stage. In this stage, stress
is very prone in me but I think that's just normal because
I am starting to adjust to adulthood which means more
weighed things, experiences and challenges are waiting
for me. Today, I am really enjoying my life because I know
that I cannot turn back time but hence, I am indeed proud
that my parents was successful in nurturing me because
I've grown into a young lady that is goal-oriented, god-fearing and responsible.
